About Barrels and Buckets

Barrels and Buckets helps buyers source industrial containers and helps suppliers move surplus inventory with a practical, relationship-driven approach.

Georgia-based, relationship-driven

Barrels and Buckets is a Georgia-based supply and distribution company focused on plastic and metal industrial containers. We help buyers source new and surplus packaging with a practical, inquiry-driven approach built around current availability, fit, freight, and fast response.

Through a network of manufacturers, distributors, recyclers, warehouses, and secondary-market partners, we support customers across North America looking for quality containers at competitive pricing. When suppliers need to move excess, closeout, or slow-moving inventory, we provide a secondary outlet that helps protect their primary sales channels.

The goal is simple: help customers lower container costs, improve supply chain flexibility, free up floor space, and turn underused inventory back into working capital. Done right, that also means less waste and a more efficient flow of usable packaging back into the market.
